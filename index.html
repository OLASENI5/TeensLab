Purpose & mission

A pyramid of hope for every young person.

We exist to foster the holistic growth, well‑being and empowerment of young people — so they become stable, fulfil their purpose, and go on to lift the lives of others around them.

  • KnowledgeEducational and learning growth that builds a strong foundation.
  • SkillsCapacity‑building programs that turn potential into livelihood.
  • IntelligenceMental development through critical thinking and challenge.
  • StabilityEmotional resilience, leadership, and financial freedom.

"Deal with it from the grassroots — and equip the next generation to change the narrative."

The TeensLab conviction

The challenge

Too many young people are left out of the solution.

Across our communities, teens and youth face cybercrime, addiction, exploitation and violence — driven by peer pressure, hard environments and a lack of direction. Lately we see it most as social anxiety, depression, loan‑app debt and poor time management. National efforts often skip the grassroots, leaving the very generation being born into these problems without a path out.

Social anxiety Depression Loan‑app debt Peer pressure Addiction Distraction Dysfunctional homes

Our approach

Three stages. One rising path.

Our framework spans ages 9–23 — meeting each young person where they are and raising them, step by step, toward purpose and self‑sufficiency.

1 EARLY BIRDS 09–12

Introduction & exposure

Discover each child's uniqueness and channel their strengths.

  • Intelligence & mental‑tasking games
  • Academic support & reading culture
  • STEM projects, art & creativity
  • Sport, field trips & mentorship
2 EXPLORERS 13–19

Exploration & discovery

Help teens discover what they're wired for, with purpose.

  • Skills & entrepreneurial training
  • Financial & digital literacy
  • Problem‑solving & personal development
  • Mentorship, internships & rescue programs
3 BUILDERS 20–23

Development & establishment

Stabilise, establish, and give back to the community.

  • Leadership & empowerment training
  • Entrepreneurial exploration
  • Personal branding & public speaking
  • Internships, volunteering & community projects

HopeEd Initiative

Quality education for missionary & IDP children.

In conflict‑affected Northern Nigeria, displaced and missionary children face insecurity, poverty and exclusion. HopeEd delivers safe, inclusive primary–secondary education — and a future.

SDG 4 · Quality Education SDG 1 · No Poverty SDG 5 · Gender Equality SDG 10 · Reduced Inequalities SDG 16 · Peace & Strong Institutions

Key activities

  • School construction & rehabilitation
  • Teacher recruitment & training
  • Learning materials provision
  • Child safeguarding & psychosocial support
  • Monitoring & evaluation

Expected impact

  • Improved access, retention & learning outcomes
  • Reduced child vulnerability to exploitation
  • Stronger, more resilient communities
  • A measurable, scalable model for partners
